ASSASSIN

The Christians are establishing churches all over Italy. Alexandro the assassin is trying to prevent the Christianity with all his force as a deadly assassin. Alex as he’s know as, is forging an army to take down the Pope and his disciples.

Play as Alexandro in the Open World game Assassin the first order, developed by Infinity Ward. Game Hub and Infinity Ward has in collaboration made the game available trough Game Box. Which means that it’s compatible with any computer.

Features

  • 4K Resolution
  • 240 FPS
  • Surround sound
  • compatible with M&K and Controller
  • VR Gameplay
  • Autosave
